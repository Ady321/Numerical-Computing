 The need for large scale computation predates the invention of modern 
 computers.            
 
 After Computers' Invention
                        
  The revolution was sparked by invention of computer.
  • By Moore's Law, the computational ability is increasing 
exponentially,doubling approximately every two years.
  • Mathematics and algorithms have also developed after 1950s.

  
 These two effects generated a combined increase in speed of almost 
 incomprehensible scale.
 
  • Half a century on, numerical computation has grown into one of the 
largest branches of mathematics.
  • Today, we have reached a point where most of the classical
mathematical problems can be solved numerically to high
accuracy.
